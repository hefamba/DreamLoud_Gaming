Chris Crane also known as "Monte" is the Founder of DreamLoud Gaming. It started with his passion for video games in the late 90's as a kid, as he would play classics like Mario Kart 64, Legends of Zelda, Sonic the Hedgehog and Doom. Graduating as an economics major in college Monte had a dream for creating a entertainment company. Starting as just YouTube videos, he quickly gained a following every time he posted about Halo. From there DreamLoud was created and was able to get big investors Such as Spongebob Squarepants, and Bruce Wayne to get on board. Within just one year Monte was able to make DreamLoud the most Notable Gaming org in the world.
