StaticShakaZulu

Obinna Iloani is another member of Team DreamLoud. A fighting game specialist, Obi has been placing at high level tournaments in Tekken Tag 2, Tekken 6, Tekken 7, MKX, Injustice 2, MK11, Sm4sh and Ultimate consistently since 2016 even before being scouted by DreamLoud coaches. He loves a good shooter and regularly plays Apex, Halo, CoD, Destiny and Gears in his downtime. His favorite Halo weapons are the Needler and the Gravity Hammer.

"Look I may not be the best, but I am definitely the smartest. And trust me you don't want to see me in Tekken 6! Been there done that!"

- StaticShakaZulu as he was interviewed by Esports Daily
